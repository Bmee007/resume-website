# Borina Keo — Resume Website

## What This Is

A dark, modern personal brand website for Borina Keo — an ERP/WMS implementation leader specializing in AI integration. The site functions as a professional portfolio and resume, targeting both potential consulting clients and full-time employers who need someone to lead AI-enhanced ERP/WMS transformations.

## Core Value

Position Borina Keo as the go-to leader for integrating AI into existing ERP (Microsoft Dynamics 365) and WMS (Manhattan DFIO) systems — converting visitors into inbound leads or interview requests.
